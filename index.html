Instructions

How to play:

  1. All cards are laid face down on the board.
  2. Click on any two cards to flip them over and reveal their images.
  3. If the cards match, they stay face up and you earn a point.
  4. If they do not match, they will flip back over after a short delay.
  5. Keep flipping two cards at a time until all pairs have been matched.
  6. The game ends when all the card pairs are found. Try to match them all in the fewest number of attempts!
